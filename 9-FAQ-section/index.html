FAQ Section

1. What if i leave the course in between.

Software Development Courses Unit 1: You have to pay us nothing to drop out of Unit 1 and if you think that Masai is not for you. Unit 2-4: If you leave our course between these units, you are liable to pay a sum of INR 50,000/- Unit 5-6: If you leave our course between the last 2 units, you have to pay the full ISA amount for the course.
